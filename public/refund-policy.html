There are two ways for you to cancel the subscription and get a refund: right to cancellation based on our policy

CANCELLATION BASED ON OUR POLICY
Cancellation & refund methods differ based on the way you have subscribed to the App.
1. If you have purchased subscription via our website (https://quiz.kegel.men/)
We currently offer a 30-Day Money Back Guarantee on the initial purchase for our membership plans.
To be eligible for a refund, we require a request to cancel your membership within 30 days of the initial
purchase. If you have purchased a subscription directly via the https://quiz.kegel.men/ website, simply
reach out to our support team at contact@kegel.men and they will happily assist you with the refund.
Following the receipt of your refund application, we will notify you by email if it is approved. We will use
your original payment method for the refund. Typically, it takes around 14 days to process the refund.
The subscription must be cancelled no less than 24 hours before its renewal date.
Please be aware that your purchased subscription does not cancel automatically if you delete the App.
You should cancel the subscription before deletion of the App.
2. If you have purchased a subscription via any app distribution platform (App Store, Google Play, etc.)
You can cancel your subscription with your app distribution platform. In order to do this, reach out to
Apple or Google (or other applicable app distribution platform) directly with any refund requests. For
example, Apple is typically able to honor all refund requests within 30 days of the purchase date (at
their discretion). Customers who subscribed to our App through App Store will need to reach out to
Apple directly at https://getsupport.apple.com/. Customers who subscribed to our App through Google
Play will need to follow instructions at https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637.
CANCELLATION DURING COOLING-OFF PERIOD
National laws may grant customers cancellation and refund rights during the so-called "cooling off period" and
we respect your rights in this regard. Conditions vary by country, but here are some examples.
For example, if you live in Poland, you have the right to cancel your subscription and request a refund without
giving any reason within 14 days from the date you started your subscription. The indicated periods are only
indicative, in this regard, the applicable laws of a given country always apply.
